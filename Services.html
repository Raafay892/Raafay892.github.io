Our Outstanding Services

  1. Individual Counseling: Rabiya believes in providing a safe and non-judgmental space for individuals to explore their emotions, concerns, and life challenges. Through evidence-based therapeutic approaches, she helps clients gain self-awareness, coping skills, and resilience to overcome obstacles and lead fulfilling lives.
  2. Career Counseling: Rabiya's expertise in career counseling allows her to guide clients in making well-informed decisions about their professional paths. Whether they are students exploring career options, mid-career professionals seeking a change, or individuals looking to enhance their performance, Rabiya offers personalized guidance and practical solutions.
  3. Psychological Assessments: Rabiya conducts comprehensive psychological assessments to identify and understand clients' strengths, weaknesses, and personality traits. These assessments assist in formulating personalized treatment plans and career development strategies.
  4. Workshops and Seminars: Alongside individual counseling, Rabiya conducts workshops and seminars on various topics related to mental health, career development, and personal growth. These interactive sessions aim to empower participants with valuable skills and knowledge to thrive in their personal and professional lives.
